Egusi Soup

How To Cook Egusi Soup

Egusi soup is an exotic hearty food made from melon that will satisfy your taste buds. It is a staple in most West African homes and this Egusi soup recipe is insanely delicious and easy to make

How To Cook Egusi Soup in Simple Steps

  1. Place a big pan on medium heat, add palm oil and heat for about 3 minutes (Do not bleach oil) add the reserved chopped onions and Sautee till translucent
  2. Add pepper mix, locust beans and stir to combine. Bring to boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the egusi paste in bits to pepper, reduce the heat, do not stir and cover the pot with a lid. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  4. Remove the lid, and gently stir the soup. The egusi would be lumpy at this point, use the back of a ladle to break the lumps it into desired size/texture
  5. Add smoked turkey, ground crayfish and crayfish, beef stock (if using) and stir to combine. Add bouillon cubes, taste and adjust accordingly.
